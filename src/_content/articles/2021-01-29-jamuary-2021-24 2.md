## Disclaimer

Building thing has never been my strong. I understand nothing to electricity.

## Why building a DIY case

### More hp is needed

All my case are full and I need more hp. 

### Building something with your hands is cool

The joy to plug the modules you have soldered and see it working is incredible. You can be proud of yourself and the thing you have builded earn some kind of emotional value.

I am not sure I will ever sold the first module I have soldered.

### You learn new skills

Thanks to Eurorack, I have learned to soldered. I am not mastering this art but now, I am able to fix some toys of my kids.

I have already build a case. It is a 3U 104hp wooden case. By doing this project, I have learned a few things:

- to glue wood together,
- taking some more `mm` in your mesures is a good idea,

## The case

### Specificity and purpose of the case

Here are the specificity of the case:

- 104 hp width
- 12 U composed of four 3U rows ans two 1U
- It won’t be a portable one

### The tools and ressources

#### Case

The case will be build using 18mm width beech wood. This is the type of wood I used to build my first case and it works well. The case looks nice and do not need to be paint. Using some wood oil will make it looks nice.

To know what to order, I used [Shapr3D](https://www.shapr3d.com) app to design the case and know how many wood I have to order.

#### Rails

I will use two [MDLR case](https://www.mdlrcase.com) 7U 104hp frames.

#### Power supply

Electricity is not my thing. I do not like that. I do understand it. Yeah I know but it’s like that. This is not my thing.

This is why I decided to use a dedicated power supply for Eurorack instead of using a cheap Meanwell and burn the house.
It will not be as cheap as a ow cost Meanwell but it will be more error proof.

I learned about [KonstantLab](https://www.konstantlab.audio) power supply in the [Feeding the Tardis](https://youtu.be/1ZQx8asaPkM) video by Divkid ([@Divkid](https://www.twitter.com/DivKid))and Chris Meyer ([@LearningModular](https://www.twitter.com/LearningModular)) where the speek about it.

I contacted Pavel from KonstantLab to ask which power supply is the best for my project. After some very friendly emails, I ordered the new Hammer set.

I can’t recommend enough KonstantLab. They are very friendly and will kindly help you for your project.

### The builds

After having all the ressources, It takes two days to have the case builded and sitting in my studio.
